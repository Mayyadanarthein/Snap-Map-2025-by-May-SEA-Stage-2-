About This Project

This project was created for Snap Engineering Academy. It showcases interactive mapping and filtering capabilities to explore snack recipes from different continents.

I built this site using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to demonstrate my front-end development skills, particularly working with arrays and objects to manage and manipulate data.

The interactive world map allows you to explore snacks from different regions with just a click. You can also search, filter, and sort recipes based on your preferences.

Feel free to explore the map, search for recipes, and even add your own favorite snacks to share with others!
